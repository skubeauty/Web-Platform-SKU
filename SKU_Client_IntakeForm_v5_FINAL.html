About SKU Beauty & the DIS Framework

Structural diagnostics that give leadership a clear picture of launch readiness — before decisions become difficult to reverse.

SKU Beauty is a product development governance partner. We work with beauty brands to strengthen the operational, financial, and structural systems that determine whether a product reaches market successfully — and whether it performs when it does.

The SKU Deviation Intelligence System™ (DIS) is a proprietary diagnostic framework that evaluates the stability of a launch environment across five integrated operational domains. DIS surfaces where structural pressure is building, where the conditions for launch failure are forming, and where leadership intervention will have the greatest impact.

The purpose of this assessment is to provide leadership with a clear, structured view of the launch environment — so that the right decisions get made at the right time, with the right information in hand.
