Our Story

We watched
brilliant ideas
die at the door.

SKU Beauty was born out of a simple belief: there has to be a better way to bring a new beauty product to market without the guesswork that derails so many good ideas.

Before founding SKU Beauty, our team worked deep inside the industry — managing formulation pipelines, negotiating with manufacturers, navigating retailer compliance requirements that appeared late and changed everything. Watching launch after launch stumble, not because the product wasn't good enough, but because no one was holding the whole process together.

We built SKU Beauty to be exactly what was missing. Not another agency layer. A partner who thinks like an operator — because we were operators, and still are.
